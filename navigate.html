This is information of delivery page

Home delivery refers to deliveries of goods to the customers’ homes (or another location of the customers’ choice such as workplaces) rather than customers having to collect the goods in-person from a physical point of sale. In home delivery operations, therefore, the physical distribution of the goods from the point of purchase to the customer needs to be organized and carried out by the seller or specialized .
